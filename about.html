Our Mission

Our aim is to develop & nurture competitive attitude amongst student. We empower you to stay ahead a step in life by offering qualitative teaching. At Diksha IAS, we believe that “Geniuses are made, not born”. They can be made with sheer commitment, dogged determination in tandem with qualitative guidance and practice. We prepare you for the future.

Aims & Objectives

Since the establishment of Diksha IAS, we have followed the modest culture of not commercializing our results but for the sole inspiration of novices we present a list of selected candidates belonging to humble background yet achieving laurels in this most prestigious exam in last pages.

Strategy

To assist the aspirants to frame an accurate and separate strategy and plan at every level of examination, i.e., preliminary, main and Interview.

Classroom Programme

Classroom Programme by the experts of their respective field, in order to assist aspirants to develop the clear and basic fundamentals of the subject.

Society

To focus at the aspirants belongs to the weaker section of the society and help them to be the part of the mainstream of the society.

Economically

To provide every assistance possible for those who belongs to the economically weaker sections of the society
